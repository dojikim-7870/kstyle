How to Practice Effectively

1

Daily Practice

Generate 5-10 phrases daily and practice pronouncing them aloud. Consistency is key to language learning.

2

Context Learning

Don't just memorize phrases - understand when and how to use them in real conversations.

3

Spaced Repetition

Review previously generated phrases regularly to reinforce memory and improve long-term retention.

4

Practice Speaking

Say each phrase out loud multiple times. Speaking practice is essential for building confidence.

5

Create Sentences

Try to create your own sentences using the phrases you've learned to practice application.

6

Use in Context

Look for opportunities to use these phrases in K-pop lyrics, dramas, or conversations.

Phrase Categories

Our phrases are organized into practical categories to help you focus on specific aspects of Korean communication.

👋

Greetings (인사)

Essential greeting phrases for different times of day and social situations.

안녕하세요 잘 지내세요 반갑습니다
🙏

Politeness (예의)

Courteous expressions for showing respect and good manners in Korean society.

감사합니다 죄송해요 괜찮아요
😊

Emotions (감정)

Express your feelings and understand others' emotional expressions in Korean.

행복해요 보고 싶어요 사랑해요
💪

Encouragement (격려)

Motivational phrases to cheer someone on or encourage yourself and others.

화이팅! 힘내세요 잘했어요
🏠

Daily Life (일상)

Common phrases used in everyday situations and routine conversations.

배고파요 피곤해요 재미있어요
💬

Communication (의사소통)

Essential phrases for effective communication and understanding in Korean.

이해해요 궁금해요 도와주세요
