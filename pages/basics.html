Learn Hangul (한글)

Hangul is the Korean alphabet. It is one of the most scientific and easy-to-learn writing systems in the world. Mastering it is the first step to fluency!

Essential Phrases

Start your conversations with these basic, must-know phrases.

안녕하세요
[annyeonghaseyo]
Hello / How are you?
감사합니다
[gamsahamnida]
Thank you
죄송합니다
[joesonghamnida]
I'm sorry
네
[ne]
Yes
아니요
[aniyo]
No
괜찮아요
[gwaenchanayo]
It's okay / I'm fine
이름이 뭐예요?
[ireumi mwoyeyo?]
What is your name?
반가워요
[banggawoyo]
Nice to meet you
잘 가요
[jal gayo]
Goodbye (to person leaving)
