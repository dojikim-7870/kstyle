1. What is this website about?

Our website provides a collection of easy-to-use online tools designed to help you complete everyday tasks more efficiently. Whether you need to calculate, convert, edit images, or generate text, our platform offers a variety of solutions in one place.

2. Are the tools free to use?

Yes! All tools on our website are completely free for personal and commercial use. We support the site through display ads, allowing us to keep everything accessible at no cost to users.

3. Do I need to create an account?

No registration or login is required. You can start using any tool instantly.

4. Are my files safe?

Uploaded files are processed locally in your browser whenever possible. When temporary uploads are required, your files are deleted automatically after processing.

5. Why are there ads on the site?

Ads help us maintain hosting, development, and operational costs while providing free access to tools.

6. Can I request a new feature?

Absolutely! Contact us anytime via the Contact Page.

7. Do you store personal information?

We do not collect personal data. Only anonymous usage analytics may be used to improve the website.

8. Which browsers are supported?

Our site works best on Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari.

9. Can I share your tools on my website?

You may link to our tools freely, but embedding or redistributing the tools is not allowed.

10. How can I stay updated?

We regularly add new tools. Check back on the homepage anytime.
